üîß Troubleshooting

Camera/Scanning Issues

1 Camera Permission: Ensure the app has camera access in your device settings. Go to Settings > Privacy > Camera (iOS) or Settings > Apps > YuGiOh Scanner > Permissions (Android).
2 Lighting Conditions: Use good lighting when scanning. Natural light or bright indoor lighting works best. Avoid shadows and glare on the card surface.
3 Card Position: Hold the card flat and fill most of the camera frame. Ensure the entire card is visible and not cut off at the edges.
4 Card Condition: Very damaged, worn, or heavily played cards may be harder to scan. Try scanning a cleaner version of the same card if available.

App Performance Issues

1 Restart the App: Close the app completely and reopen it. On iOS, swipe up and swipe away the app. On Android, use the recent apps button.
2 Check Internet Connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection for scanning and price updates. Try switching between WiFi and mobile data.
3 Update the App: Make sure you're using the latest version from your app store. Updates often include performance improvements and bug fixes.
4 Free Up Storage: Ensure your device has sufficient storage space. Low storage can cause app performance issues.

Deck Building Issues

üí° Tip: Yu-Gi-Oh decks must follow official tournament rules:
  • Main Deck: 40-60 cards
  • Extra Deck: 0-15 cards
  • Side Deck: 0-15 cards
  • Maximum 3 copies of any card (except Limited/Semi-Limited)

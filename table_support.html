About Snake Math

Snake Math is an educational game that helps elementary students learn multiplication tables through engaging gameplay. Guide your snake to eat correct answers while avoiding wrong ones in this fun math adventure.

Age Range: 7-12 years old
Educational Focus: Multiplication tables 1-20
Platform: iOS and Android devices

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Snake Math?
Swipe to move your snake around the grid. Solve the multiplication problem shown at the top, then guide your snake to eat the correct answer. Avoid eating wrong answers, as this will end the game.
What is the difference between Easy and Expert modes?
Easy mode provides visual hints after 3 seconds - correct answers glow green while wrong answers remain neutral. Expert mode shows no hints, requiring pure math knowledge to identify correct answers.
How do I unlock new multiplication tables?
Complete the current table by getting 10 consecutive correct answers. Once completed, the next table will automatically unlock. Tables 1 and 2 are unlocked by default.
Does my child's progress save?
Yes, all progress is automatically saved to your device. Completed tables, best times, and unlocked content are preserved between sessions.
Is this app suitable for my child's grade level?
Snake Math is designed for elementary students typically in grades 2-6 (ages 7-12) who are learning multiplication tables. The difficulty progresses from simple tables to more challenging ones.

Educational Guide for Parents and Teachers

How to Use Snake Math Effectively

  • Start with Easy mode to build confidence
  • Practice one table at a time before moving to the next
  • Encourage daily 10-15 minute practice sessions
  • Switch to Expert mode once comfortable with Easy mode
  • Review completed tables periodically to maintain skills

Educational Benefits

  • Builds multiplication fluency through repetition
  • Improves mental math speed and accuracy
  • Makes math practice engaging and enjoyable
  • Provides immediate feedback on answers
  • Tracks progress and celebrates achievements

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

The game is running slowly or lagging
Close other apps running in the background and restart Snake Math. Ensure your device has sufficient storage space available.
My progress was lost
Progress is saved automatically to your device. If progress is lost, check that you haven't accidentally reset the app data or reinstalled the app.
The snake is not responding to swipes
Make sure you're swiping clearly in the direction you want to move. The snake cannot immediately reverse direction, so swipe in a perpendicular direction first.
I can't hear sound effects
Check your device's volume settings and ensure the mute switch is off. Some sound effects use the device's haptic feedback system.

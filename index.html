Frequently Asked Questions

Subscription & Billing

Q: How do I restore my purchase?
A: Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → US Citizenship Test Trainer → Cancel Subscription.

Technical Issues

Q: The app crashes when I start practice sessions
A: Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.

Q: AI feedback is not working
A: This is a premium feature. Ensure you have an active subscription and internet connection.

Practice & Features

Q: I've reached my daily session limit
A: Free users get 3 sessions per day. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited practice sessions.

Q: CarPlay is not working
A: Ensure your car supports CarPlay and your iPhone is connected. Go to Listen Mode in the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Subscription & Billing

Q: How do I restore my purchase?
A: Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → US Citizenship Test Trainer → Cancel Subscription.

Q: What's included in the Premium subscription?
A: Premium includes unlimited practice sessions, AI-powered feedback on your responses, detailed performance analytics, and access to all question categories.

Technical Issues

Q: The app crashes when I start practice sessions
A: Try restarting the app and ensuring you have the latest version. If the issue persists, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.

Q: AI feedback is not working
A: This is a premium feature. Ensure you have an active subscription and stable internet connection. AI evaluation requires network access to process your responses.

Q: Video interview questions won't load
A: Check your internet connection and try again. Videos are cached for offline practice once initially loaded.

Practice & Features

Q: I've reached my daily session limit
A: Free users get 3 sessions per day. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited practice sessions and advanced features.

Q: How does the AI interview coaching work?
A: Our AI analyzes your spoken responses to interview questions, providing personalized feedback on content, delivery, and areas for improvement.

Q: Can I practice offline?
A: Some features work offline after initial download, but AI evaluation and new content require an internet connection.

Q: CarPlay is not working
A: Ensure your car supports CarPlay and your iPhone is connected. Go to Listen Mode in the app for audio-only practice sessions.

Audio & Recording

Q: My audio recordings aren't being saved
A: Check that you've granted microphone permissions to the app in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

Q: Can I delete my recorded responses?
A: Yes, you can delete individual recordings from your practice history or contact support for bulk deletion.

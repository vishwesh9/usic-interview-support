Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Privacy Overview

Child-Safe Design: Snake Math is designed with children's privacy and safety as our top priority. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of any age.

This Privacy Policy explains how Snake Math ("we," "our," or "the app") handles information when you use our educational mobile application. Snake Math is committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Snake Math is designed to be completely private. We do not collect any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Age, birth date, or demographic information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, videos, or camera access
  • Microphone or audio recordings
  • Contacts or phone book information
  • Social media profiles or account information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking

Data Storage

Local Device Storage Only

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Game progress and completed multiplication tables
  • Best completion times and achievements
  • Difficulty settings and preferences
  • Unlocked content and current level
Important: This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third parties. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

Internet and Network Usage

Offline Operation

Snake Math operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection to play. The app does not:

  • Connect to our servers or any external servers
  • Send or receive data over the internet
  • Access network resources
  • Communicate with other apps or services

Third-Party Services

Snake Math does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Crash reporting tools
No Ads: Snake Math contains no advertisements and does not work with advertising partners.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Snake Math is designed for children ages 7-12 and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We take the following measures to protect children's privacy:

  • No personal information collection from users of any age
  • No account creation or registration required
  • No communication features or social interactions
  • No ability to share information outside the app
  • No behavioral tracking or profiling
  • Complete offline operation
For Parents: You can feel confident that your child's privacy is completely protected while using Snake Math. No personal information can be collected or shared because the app has no network connectivity.

Permissions

Required Permissions

Snake Math requests minimal permissions necessary for operation:

iOS Permissions:

  • Local Storage: To save game progress on your device

Android Permissions:

  • Vibration: For haptic feedback during gameplay (optional)
  • Storage: To save game progress on your device

We do not request or use permissions for camera, microphone, location, contacts, or internet access.

Data Security

Since Snake Math does not collect or transmit any data, traditional data security concerns do not apply. However, we ensure security through:

  • No network connections or data transmission
  • Local-only data storage using device security
  • No external access points or vulnerabilities
  • Regular security reviews of app code

International Users

Snake Math can be used worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, international privacy laws such as GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) are not applicable. The app provides the same privacy protection regardless of your location.

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through app store update descriptions
  • Maintain our commitment to not collecting personal information

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

Since no personal data is collected, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are not applicable. However, you maintain complete control over your experience:

  • All game data stays on your device under your control
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • You can reset progress through the app's settings
  • No external accounts or profiles are created

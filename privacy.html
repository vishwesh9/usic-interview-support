Privacy Policy for US Citizenship Test Trainer

Last Updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and support
  • Practice Data: Your progress, scores, and study statistics
  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings for pronunciation practice (stored temporarily)
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized study recommendations
  • Track your learning progress
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscription payments via Apple App Store

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Apple: Subscription and payment processing
  • Service Providers: Cloud storage and analytics (anonymized data only)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Audio recordings are processed and deleted within 24 hours
  • Practice data is stored securely on our servers
  • We use industry-standard security measures

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Export your study progress data
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • AI Services: Answer evaluation (data not stored)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
